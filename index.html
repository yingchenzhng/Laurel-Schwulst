My Website is a Shifting House next to a River of Knowledge. What could yours be?

Laurel Schwultz

What is a Website?

A website is a beautifully crafted collection of digital experiences hosted on remote servers.

  • A server, always connected to the internet, breathes life into a website.
  • A domain name, an elegant piece of language, directs users to this digital masterpiece.
  • An IP address, a string of numbers, serves as its secret address.

The Duality of Website:

They are both subject and object at once. A website creator becomes both author and architect, shaping digital worlds.

Why make a Website?

They empower creators to fashion not only digital creations but entire immersive experiences, with infinite possibilities.

What can a Website be?

  • As a room: it is a finite, purposeful space.
  • As a shelf: a curated collection of digital artifacts.
  • As a plant: growing organically over time.
  • As a garden: ever-changing, tending to ideas.
  • As a puddle: a transient reflection of creative thoughts.
  • As a thrown rock that's now falling deep into the ocean: a creation released into the vast digital world.
